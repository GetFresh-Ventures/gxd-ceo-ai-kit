## Design Principles

1. **Resolve, don't summarize** тАФ Every skill should DO work, not just report
2. **Source of truth or bust** тАФ Always ground in live systems, never memory alone
3. **One variable per experiment** тАФ From `experiment-loop`, but applies everywhere
4. **Not handled until in the system** тАФ Reading тЙа Processing
5. **Safe auto-resolve, draft-first for judgment** тАФ From `chief-of-staff`'s auto-resolver
6. **Simplicity criterion** тАФ Same result + simpler approach = keep simpler
7. **Voice-native** тАФ Everything sounds like the CEO, not like AI
8. **Draft Review Before Send** тАФ No outbound communication without CEO approval
9. **Hierarchical topology only** тАФ No ring/mesh patterns that cause agent drift (from ruflo)
10. **One brain, many hands** тАФ Orchestrator plans, specialists execute (from ruflo)
