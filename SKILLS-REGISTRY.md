## Design Principles

1. **Resolve, don't summarize** ‚Äî Every skill should DO work, not just report
2. **Source of truth or bust** ‚Äî Always ground in live systems, never memory alone
3. **One variable per experiment** ‚Äî From `experiment-loop`, but applies everywhere
4. **Not handled until in the system** ‚Äî Reading ‚âÝ Processing
5. **Safe auto-resolve, draft-first for judgment** ‚Äî From `chief-of-staff`'s auto-resolver
6. **Simplicity criterion** ‚Äî Same result + simpler approach = keep simpler
7. **Voice-native** ‚Äî Everything sounds like Diraj, not like AI
